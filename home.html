Context

The aim of this visualization assignment is to produce a visualization to display
information regarding the migration patterns of healthcare workers, to identify
whether there are patterns or preferred locations. Additionally, to identify whether
there were any changes to the preference before and after covid-19.

Target Audience

This visualization is targeted towards students in the healthcare sector so that they
can use this information to find out which are the preferred locations to pursue their
career and as a starting point for that research. Another target user which could
use this information is policy makers as this can assist them in seeing which countries
are attracting most of their healthcare workforce and try to identify the reasons.
